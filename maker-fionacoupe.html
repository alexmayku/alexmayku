Silicone quality custom molds in 5 minutes

Fiona Coupe is a Freelance Prop Designer and Homewares Maker for her Jesmonite brand, Nona Living. Fiona used the Multiplier to make custom molds for a table, lamp base and board game design cast in Jesmonite and Resin.

About Fiona Coupe

Bold homewares with creative materials

Fiona began her creative career in visual merchandising, quickly expanding onwards into prop making, sculpture and art installations. With a client list including Anthropologie, Saatchi and Saatchi, Selfridges and Jo Malone, Fiona was inspired to create more and Nona Living was born during lockdown. Fiona’s mission? To create homewares accessories inspired by a passion for exploring materials and texture.

Process

How Fiona Coupe uses the Mayku Multiplier

Fiona started with sketches which were then transformed into digital files by Mayku Lead Creator, Agustín Arroyo.

The templates were manufactured with a mixture of laser cut wood and 3D printing ready to form on the Multiplier.

Each mold takes less than 5 minutes to make and is immediately ready to work with. The Multiplier creates crisp edges to shapes for silicone mold quality production.

Benefits

What Fiona Loves about the Mayku Multiplier

Creativity

Greater creative flexibility

Test ideas rapidly and without outsourcing whilst developing a unique brand identity. Work with a variety of sheet materials to keep costs low and creativity flowing. Quick, affordable 2mm molds can be tested immediately before committing to making a Flexible mold on the same machine, ready for your batch production.

About the machine

Ease

Easier than custom silicone molds

Creating custom silicone molds is a complex skill to master. By using the Flexible sheets on the Multiplier, a custom mold can be made in 5 minutes with no time wasted mixing or curing. Cleaning up is as simple as closing the lid of the machine.

About the machine

Profit

Improved profit margins and productivity

Unique custom molds help a brand stand out from the crowd, improving your profit potential and point of difference. By taking the entire mold production process in-house with the help of the Multiplier, designers and makers can save money and time compared to other custom mold methods.
