Process

How Fiona Coupe uses the Mayku Multiplier

Fiona started with sketches which were then transformed into digital files by Mayku Lead Creator, Agustín Arroyo.

The templates were manufactured with a mixture of laser cut wood and 3D printing ready to form on the Multiplier.

Each mold takes less than 5 minutes to make and is immediately ready to work with. The Multiplier creates crisp edges to shapes for silicone mold quality production.

Ease

Easier than custom silicone molds

Creating custom silicone molds is a complex skill to master. By using the Flexible sheets on the Multiplier, a custom mold can be made in 5 minutes with no time wasted mixing or curing. Cleaning up is as simple as closing the lid of the machine.
