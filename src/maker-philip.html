Why the Multiplier?

Mold bigger, faster and more detailed

Speed

A pro workflow for rapid production

  • Manufacture small to medium sized projects without outsourcing
  • Reduce lead times by taking manufacturing in house
  • Multiply custom molds and shapes in minutes

Precision

A pro workflow for rapid production

  • Four times more precise than vacuum forming
  • Capture tiny details as small as 0.0003mm for injection molding quality
  • Work with a wide variety of materials from 0.1mm - 10mm in sheet thickness

Control

A pro workflow for rapid production

  • Choose to reduce or increase the pressure to control how much detail to capture
  • Work with a wide variety of thermoplastics including Polycarbonate and Flexible sheets
  • Monitor manufacturing from a high contrast LCD screen with smart updates
