How It Works

What is Pressure Forming?

Pressure forming is an industrial grade manufacturing process, used to make molds and parts.

Pressure forming can capture detail finer than a human hair and perfectly sharp edges, for super precise forms and molds.

With increased accuracy, pressure forming allows users to reach injection molding levels of detail right from their desktop.
